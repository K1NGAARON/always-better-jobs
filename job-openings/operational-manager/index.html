Operational Manager

Always Better is growing and we are looking for a Director of Operations to organise and oversee the daily operations of our business unit. You will work to ensure the growth and long-term success of the business, while coaching and taking care of the people. The ideal candidate must be an excellent leader with very strong operational skills, always trying to discover the most effective way to run the business.

Jouw profiel

  • You're between 27-35y old.
  • You're passionate about health & wellbeing.
  • You value trust. When you commit to something, you deliver. You expect the same from the people you work with.
  • You go the extra mile and never give up. You're self-motivated, hard working and don't like showing off.
  • You're a team player. People describe you as positive, respectful, friendly and a good listener.
  • You're high-energy and always up for a challenge.
  • You're not afraid to ask questions or challenge assumptions.
  • You respect people and expect the same in return.
  • You're professional, output driven and like to work with focused, self-motivated individuals.
  • You're emotionally intelligent and value transparent, open communication.
  • You have excellent organisational management skills.
  • You are (self) educated in business administration or relevant fields; engineering or analytical background is a plus.
  • You have an in-depth knowledge of diverse business functions and principles (supply chain, finance, customer service, HR).

Je takenpakket als operationeel manager:

  • Serving as the bridge between senior management and the operational business staff. Working on both strategic goals as well as the day-to-day implementation.
  • Overseeing recruitment and employment decisions at business unit level. Providing direction, coaching and delivering constructive feedback.
  • Promoting communication and cooperation to establish and maintain the Always Better culture. Creating a spirit of unity in the organisation. Ensuring a healthy work environment, both physically and emotionally.
  • Periodically reporting on the status of the company's performance. Comparing them with the objectives and taking appropriate measures if needed.

Het verhaal achter Always Better

Always Better is een health house voor ondernemers en drukbezette professionals.

Voor hen zijn wij een charging station. Een tweede thuis in de stad waar je de batterijen komt opladen zodat je er nadien weer 100% tegenaan kan gaan. Bekijk ons als een medevennoot voor je gezondheid.

Wat kan je verwachten?

  • Veel uitdaging en persoonlijke groei.
  • Competitief verloningspakket.
  • Doorgroeimogelijkheden.
  • Een positieve omgeving waar je omringd wordt door gedreven individu's die de beste versie van zichzelf willen worden.
